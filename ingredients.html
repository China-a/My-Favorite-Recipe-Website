Ingredients For Hibachi Chicken

Yum Yum Sauce

  • 1 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp melted butter (unsalted)
  • 1 tsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2-3 T water

Hibachi Chicken

  • 1 pound of chicken breast (cut in bite sized pieces)
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable or canola oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp garlic butter
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

Hibachi Rice

  • 2 cups cold, cooked rice (white rice)
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 sweet onion (chopped)
  • 1 large egg
  • chopped green onions

Hibachi Vegetables

  • 1 large zucchini (quartered)
  • 1 sweet onion (chopped)
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable or canola oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp garlic butter
  • pinch of salt

Please use the vegetable oil as needed!
